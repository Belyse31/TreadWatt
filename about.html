About Us

Pioneering Sustainable Energy Solutions

TreadWatt was born from a simple question: What if we could harness the energy we waste every day?

🎯

Our Mission

To revolutionize urban energy infrastructure by capturing kinetic energy from everyday human movement, making sustainable power generation accessible, practical, and scalable for cities worldwide.

🔭

Our Vision

A future where every step contributes to a cleaner planet—where sidewalks, gyms, and public spaces become distributed power generators, reducing carbon emissions and empowering smart cities.

Our Story

From Concept to Reality

💡

2023 – The Idea

Inspired by crowded train stations and busy gyms, our team recognized the massive potential of untapped kinetic energy. We began researching piezoelectric materials and energy harvesting systems.

🔬

Early 2024 – Research & Development

Months of testing various piezoelectric configurations, materials, and circuit designs. We partnered with university labs to validate our approach and optimize energy conversion efficiency.

🛠️

Mid 2024 – First Prototype

Successfully built our first functional tile prototype capable of generating 5-8 joules per step. Initial tests demonstrated consistent power output and durability under repeated stress.

🏆

Late 2024 – Recognition & Testing

Presented at innovation competitions and sustainability forums. Deployed pilot installations in campus walkways, collecting real-world performance data and user feedback.

🚀

2025 – Scaling Up

Currently seeking partnerships for larger deployments in urban areas, transit hubs, and fitness centers. Patent pending for our multi-layer piezoelectric array design.

The Challenge

Why Kinetic Energy Harvesting?

⚠️

Energy Crisis

Global energy demand is rising rapidly, with cities consuming 75% of the world's energy and producing 80% of greenhouse gas emissions.

🏙️

Wasted Potential

Millions of footsteps in urban areas daily represent untapped kinetic energy—enough to power street lights, sensors, and charging stations.

💰

Infrastructure Costs

Traditional grid expansion is expensive and time-consuming. Distributed micro-generation offers a scalable, cost-effective alternative.

🌍

Climate Action

Meeting carbon neutrality goals requires innovative renewable solutions beyond solar and wind—kinetic energy fills this gap.
