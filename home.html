Innovation

Transforming Motion into Power

TreadWatt is a cutting-edge kinetic energy harvesting system designed to capture wasted mechanical energy from human movement and convert it into usable electricity.

🔬

Piezoelectric Technology

Advanced piezoelectric materials generate voltage when compressed by footsteps or gym equipment motion.
